Mahjong Score Calculator (Jan 2025)
This WeChat Mini App is designed to assist players in calculating the payment points in Japanese Mahjong. By allowing users to input their hand tiles when winning, the program automatically computes the corresponding score based on the rules of Japanese Mahjong. It provides a convenient tool for both beginners and advanced players to quickly calculate points, ensuring accurate scoring and a better understanding of the game's mechanics.
Tech stack: JavaScript, CSS, WeChat Mini App

Export QQMusic PlayList (Dec 2024)
This Python script allows users to export their QQ Music playlists. By interacting with QQ Music's share link, the script retrieves a user's playlist and exports it in text file. This tool provides an easy way for QQ Music users to back up, share, or transfer their playlists to other platforms.
Tech stack: Python
